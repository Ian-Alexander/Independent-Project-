About Ian

Ian Alexander is a resident of Portland, Oregon, who utilizes a critical equity lens to approach their work in web development, design, and in life. Before graduating with a degree in Critical Race, Gender and Sexuality Studies from Humboldt State University in 2016, Ian spent two years working on small organic farms in the Pacific Northwest and Hawaii, as a route to environmental, societal, and personal connection. Ian relocated to Portland after graduating college, joining the non-profit sector to serve LGBT Seniors and the houseless population. Ian is studying web development to be a part of the legacy of LGBTQ and femme people who have created a space for themselves in the field of technology, and to create quality webpages with a conscious focus on accessability.
