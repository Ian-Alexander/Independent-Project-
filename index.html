Ian Alexander

They/Them/Theirs

Ian Alexander is a resident of Portland, Oregon, and is a person who utilizes a critical equity lens to apprach their work in web devlopment and design, as well as in evey project they undertake in work and personal life. Originally from California, Ian graduated from Humboldt State University in 2016 with a Bachelor's degree in Critical Race, Gender and Sexuality Studies. After graduating, Ian took what they leanred into the field, literally, as they managed a small scale aquaponic farm on the island of Oahu for one year before moving to Portland with the goal of setting roots of their own. Ian is studying web development to join the long legacy of queer people who have carved a space out for themsleves in the technology industry, and to create beatuiful and easy to use websites for the masses.
