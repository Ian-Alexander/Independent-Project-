About Ian

Ian Alexander is a resident of Portland, Oregon, who utilizes a critical equity lens to approach their work in web development, design, and life. Ian graduated with a degree in Critical Race, Gender and Sexuality Studies from Humboldt State University in 2016, and applied what they learned in school into the field, literally, as they worked as a farm manager of a 5-acre aquaponic farm on the island of Oahu in Hawaii. Ian relocated to Portland after a year of farm practice, in an effort to join the non-profit sector, serving LGBT Seniors and Houseless population. Ian is studying web development to be a part of the legacy of LGBTQ folks who have created a space for themselves in the technology field, and to create quality webpages with a conscious focus on diversity and equity.
